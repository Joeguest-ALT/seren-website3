About Seren

Care that puts
people first.

Seren was founded with a simple aim: to provide care that people would genuinely want for their own families.

Today, we support thousands of hours of care every week across South Wales, but our focus remains the same: do the right thing, deliver quality care, and build a team we're proud of.

Person-centred, built around each individual
Permanently employed staff, no agency workers
Directors actively involved day-to-day
