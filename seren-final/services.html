Our Services

Flexible care,
tailored to you.

From companionship to complex clinical care, we provide a full range of services designed to support people to live safely and independently in their own homes.

6 Care Services
All under one roof
CIW Registered Provider
A

Domiciliary Care

Supporting people with day-to-day living in the comfort of their own home. This includes personal care, medication support, meal preparation, and help with daily routines. Our visits are tailored to each individual, ensuring care fits around their lifestyle, not the other way around.

B

Complex Care

Supporting individuals with higher levels of need, delivered by trained and experienced staff. We provide specialist support for people with complex health conditions, working closely with healthcare professionals to ensure care is safe, consistent, and responsive.

Enquire about this service →
C

Reablement & Support

Helping people regain independence following illness, injury, or hospital discharge. Our focus is on building confidence and supporting people to get back to their normal routines, with a clear goal of promoting long-term independence.

Enquire about this service →
D

Live-in Care

Providing round-the-clock support within the comfort of your own home. Live-in care offers an alternative to residential care, with a dedicated carer providing consistent support, companionship, and reassurance throughout the day and night.

Enquire about this service →
E

Companionship & Sitting Services

Providing company, reassurance, and support when it's needed most. Whether it's a friendly visit, support to attend appointments, or sitting with a loved one to give family members a break, our team is there to offer reliable and compassionate support.

Enquire about this service →
F

Domestic & Shopping Support

Helping to keep things running smoothly at home. This includes light household tasks, laundry, and support with food shopping, helping people maintain a clean, safe, and comfortable living environment.
